Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how World Cup Story (the "App", "we", "us") handles your information. In short: we do not collect any personal data, and everything you do in the App stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

None. World Cup Story does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal information. We do not collect any data about you.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Your in-game information — such as your chosen team name, game progress, unlocked players, achievements, statistics, and settings — is stored only locally on your device using your device's standard local storage. This information never leaves your device, is never transmitted to us or any third party, and we have no access to it.

3. No Tracking, Analytics, or Advertising

The App contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking. We do not use cookies, advertising identifiers, or software development kits (SDKs) that collect or transmit your data.

4. No Sharing With Third Parties

Because we do not collect any personal data, we have nothing to sell, share, or disclose to third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. It is safe for users of all ages with respect to data privacy.

6. Player and Team Names

Footballer names, national team names, and flags shown in the App are used for identification in a non-commercial, fan context only. They are not personal data about you and are not used to identify or track you.

7. Deleting Your Data

Since all data is stored locally, you are always in control. You can erase it at any time from within the App via Settings → Reset Progress, or by uninstalling the App from your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at ertugrul_kurtul@hotmail.com.
